# Prompt: Capture C64 Learning Lab Evidence

Use this prompt after running a lab in VICE or on hardware.

Task:

```text
Convert the terminal output, screenshot description, and human observation into a Capture Back. State exactly what was tested, what was observed, whether it was emulator or hardware evidence, and what remains unproven.
```

Do not overclaim. A successful emulator run is not a real-hardware proof.
